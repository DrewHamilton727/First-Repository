Hashbrown Casserole

A dish made popular by Cracker Barrel, hashbrown casserole is sure to impress at your next brunch!

Ingredients


Quantity Ingredient
1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup
2 Cups Cheddar Cheese
2 lbs. Cubed Potatoes
1 Pint Sour Cream

Instructions

  1. Combine soup, cheese,
    potatoes, sour cream in
    a large mixing bowl
  2. Pour mixture into large
    baking dish
  3. Preheat oven to 350 F
  4. Let bake for 45 minutes
